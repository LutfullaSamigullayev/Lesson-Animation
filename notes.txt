Animation
1. keyframes 
* 0% , 100%
* from to
2. animation-name
3. animation-duration
4. animation-iteration-count
5. animation-delay
6. animation-direction:
* normal : Animatsiya oldinga qarab qo'yiladi 
* reverse : Animatsiya orqaga qarab qo'yiladi
* alternate : Animatsiya birinchi oldinga, keyin orqaga qarab qo'yiladi
* alternate-reverse: Animatsiya birinchi orqaga keyin oldinga qarab qo'yiladi 
7. animation-fill-mode  - animatsiya boshlanishidan oldin va tugagandan keyin , elementimiz o'zining oldingi xosslariga ega bo'ladi, animation-fill-mode orqali buni o'zgartirishimiz mumkin
* none: animatsiya tugaganidan so'ng , elementning oldingi xossalriga ta'sir qilamydi
* forwards: element animatsiyaning oxirgi keyframe intervali tomonidan ko'rsatilgan xosslarga ega bo'ladi
backwards: element animatsiyaning birinchi keyframe intervali tomonidan ko'rsatilgan xossalarga ega bo'ladi
* both : element animatsiyaning birinchi va oxirgi keyframe intervali tomonnidan ko'rsatilgan xossalarga ega bo'ladi
8. animation-play-state:
* running
* paused
